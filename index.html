Travel & Stay

We can't wait to share this island with you.

Getting here

Fly into Malta International Airport (MLA). From Ċirkewwa, take the Gozo Channel ferry to Mġarr (approx. 20–30 min). Taxis and buses connect easily.

Where to stay (near Kantra)

  • Hotel Ta’ Cenc & Spa — Cenc Street, Sannat SNT9049, Gozo. Tripadvisor
  • Cesca Boutique Hotel — Xlendi Valley edge, Munxar/Xlendi. Official site

Getting around

Rent a car or use taxis for flexibility; buses run frequently between towns. Summer sun is strong — pack water, hats, and SPF.
